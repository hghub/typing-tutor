## Owner Pay Is the Output, Not the Starting Point

Freelancers often treat business inflow as personal salary too early. That creates a false sense of income and leaves the business exposed when tax, client delays, or weak months arrive.

## The Better Sequence

1. separate tax reserve
2. separate operating reserve
3. keep building emergency runway
4. then decide what owner pay is actually safe

## Why This Matters

If owner pay is set too aggressively, every delayed invoice turns into stress. That usually leads to bad pricing, emergency withdrawals, or using tax money for living costs.

## What a Safer Owner-Pay Rule Does

A safer owner-pay rule protects the business first and gives you a number you can use without pretending every good month is permanent.

## When Owner Pay Can Rise

- tax reserve is consistently funded
- operating reserve is healthy
- emergency runway is close to target
- revenue stability improves over several months, not just one
