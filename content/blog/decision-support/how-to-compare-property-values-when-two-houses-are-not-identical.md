## Nearby Sale Price Does Not Automatically Mean Equal Value

People often compare a property with “a similar one nearby” and stop there. The problem is that similar rarely means identical once bedroom count, bathrooms, area, garage space, and other practical differences enter the picture.

## What a Better Comparison Tries to Do

- start from a known comparable sale
- adjust for the features that are different
- see the delta clearly instead of arguing loosely
- understand whether the asking price still makes sense
