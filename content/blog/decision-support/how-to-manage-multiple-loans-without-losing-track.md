## One Loan Is Math. Multiple Loans Become a System Problem.

An EMI calculator helps before or during one loan decision. But once you are dealing with several active loans, partial repayments, informal lending, or borrowed money across different people, the real problem becomes tracking: who is owed what, what was paid, what is still open, and which item is already overdue.

## Where People Usually Start Losing Clarity

- payments are noted in chat instead of one place
- someone remembers the original amount but not the remaining balance
- partial repayments happen with no running history
- personal and business borrowing get mixed together

## What a Loan Tracker Should Give You

- a clean balance per loan
- payment history per loan
- active vs settled status
- clear separation between money you lent and money you borrowed
