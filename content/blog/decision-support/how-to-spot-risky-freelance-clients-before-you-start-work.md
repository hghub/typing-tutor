## Freelance Problems Usually Begin Before the Work Begins

Many bad projects do not become risky halfway through. The warning signs are already there at the start: vague payment terms, weak scope, pressure to begin without clarity, or a client source that deserves more caution.

## Common Red Flags to Check Early

- unclear milestones or deliverables
- late or vague payment structure
- pressure to start before the agreement is solid
- platform or direct-client context that changes the risk
