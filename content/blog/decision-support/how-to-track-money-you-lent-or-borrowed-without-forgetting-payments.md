## Small Loans Become Confusing Faster Than People Expect

It starts simply: someone borrows a little money, you pay a part back, another amount gets added, and suddenly nobody is completely sure what the remaining balance is.

## What Usually Goes Wrong

- partial payments are remembered loosely, not recorded properly
- people mix “I lent” and “I borrowed” in one note thread
- overdue amounts stay vague until they become awkward
