## Habits Fail Quietly Before They Fail Completely

Most people do not stop because one day went badly. They stop because they lose sight of the pattern, then the break turns into a gap, and the gap turns into “I should restart someday.”

## What Helps Habits Feel Real

- a visible streak
- a monthly heatmap you can actually read
- weekly pattern feedback instead of vague guilt
- custom habits instead of someone else’s template
