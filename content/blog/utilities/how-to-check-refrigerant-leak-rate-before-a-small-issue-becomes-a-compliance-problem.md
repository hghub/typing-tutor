## Refrigerant Added Is Not Just a Service Note

In professional HVAC and refrigeration work, added refrigerant is not only a maintenance detail. It can be an early sign that the system needs a more serious compliance or repair response.

## Why This Decision Cannot Stay Vague

- the charge size matters
- the added amount matters
- thresholds matter
- repair timing can matter too
