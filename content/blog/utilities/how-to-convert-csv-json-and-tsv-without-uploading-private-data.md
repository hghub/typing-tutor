## Data Conversion Is Easy Until the Data Is Sensitive

Turning CSV into JSON or TSV into CSV is not conceptually hard. The real issue is trust. Many online converters are fine for test rows, but not for customer records, internal exports, logs, or operational data.

## What a Better Workflow Looks Like

- convert locally in the browser
- preview the output before downloading
- mask obvious identifiers when needed
- avoid sending anything to a third-party server
