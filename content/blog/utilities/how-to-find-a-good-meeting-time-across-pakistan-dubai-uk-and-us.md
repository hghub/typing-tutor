## Most Time-Zone Mistakes Are Small but Expensive

You do not need a giant calendar disaster for time-zone mistakes to hurt. One missed call, one wrong client meeting, or one early-morning family coordination problem is enough to make the issue feel bigger than it should.

## The Hardest Pattern for Pakistani Users

A common combination is Pakistan with one or more of:

- Dubai or Saudi time
- UK time
- US East or Canada time

The complexity rises further when daylight saving time shifts one region but not another.

## What Makes a Good Meeting-Time Tool Useful

- multiple live clocks side by side
- working-hours overlap, not just raw time
- quick comparison between Karachi, Dubai, London, Toronto, New York and similar cities
- a way to explain the result to someone else clearly
