## Most Packing Mistakes Are Predictable

People do not usually forget because they are careless. They forget because they pack from memory, rush the night before, and underestimate how weather and documents change what matters.

## What a Smarter List Should Consider

- the forecast for your destination
- trip length and trip type
- documents and chargers, not just clothes
- which items are reusable every trip
