## Many To-Do Lists Stay Busy but Not Useful

The problem is usually not a lack of tasks. It is that the list keeps growing while unfinished work gets copied around with no structure and no sense of what really matters today.

## What a Better Daily Planner Should Do

- focus on one day clearly
- let you assign priority and time
- show what is still pending from yesterday
- make follow-through visible across the week
