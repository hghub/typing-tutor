## Deleting Text Is Not the Same as Redacting It

Many people think they “removed” sensitive information because they cropped a screenshot or covered a line visually. Real redaction means making sure the hidden information is no longer exposed in the version you share.

## Common Things People Need to Hide

- CNIC and phone numbers
- bank details or invoice references
- client names and addresses
- confidential prices or rates
- internal comments or notes in document snapshots

## Where This Comes Up in Real Life

- sending a sample invoice to another client
- sharing a CV or document screenshot publicly
- posting a proof image in a WhatsApp group
- sending a partial contract or letter for review
