## Most People Have the Data but Not the Picture

Bank statements, Easypaisa exports, and JazzCash transaction histories already contain the truth about your spending. The problem is that raw transaction rows do not automatically tell the story clearly enough to act on.

## What a Spending Review Should Make Obvious

- which categories eat the most money
- which merchants appear too often
- how monthly spending shifts over time
- whether the feeling about spending matches the data
