## Symptoms Often Feel Random Until You See the Pattern

Many people remember the bad days but miss the pattern behind them. Was the headache worse on hotter days? Did fatigue rise after poor sleep? Did weather pressure shifts line up with pain flare-ups?

## Why Context Matters

A symptom note without context is often too thin to be useful later. A better record includes severity, timing, notes, and the surrounding conditions.

## Use a Tracker That Keeps the Context Attached

The [Symptom Context Tracker](/tools/symptom-tracker) helps log symptoms alongside local weather and pressure so you can spot patterns more clearly before discussing them with a doctor or adjusting your habits.
