## Traffic Fines Usually Become Stressful Because They Stay Half-Tracked

A single fine is simple. The problem starts when payment dates, repeated violations, and suspension risk all live in memory, screenshots, or old messages instead of one record.

## What a Better Tracker Should Keep Clear

- what happened and when
- which fines are still unpaid
- how much total liability is pending
- whether the pattern is becoming a bigger license problem
